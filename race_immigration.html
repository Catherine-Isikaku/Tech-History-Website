Impact of Racial Ideologies in the Tech Industry

Persistent Underrepresentation and Systemic Bias

Despite efforts to promote diversity, Black and Hispanic individuals remain underrepresented in the tech workforce, particularly in leadership roles. From 2005 to 2022, there was minimal change in the representation of Black workers and virtually no change for female workers in the high-tech sector.

This underrepresentation is compounded by systemic biases in hiring and promotion practices. For instance, Black tech professionals often face unequal pay and limited career progression opportunities.

Source: EEOC.gov
